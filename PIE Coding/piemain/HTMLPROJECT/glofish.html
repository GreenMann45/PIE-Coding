> What are GloFish? <

GloFish™ are genetically engineered bioluminescent fish. (Fish that were modified to glow in the dark) The original developer of these fish were Yorktown Technologies.
They got bought out by Spectrum Brands, Inc. in 2017 who now owns the rights to the GloFish™.
These fish were modified and created to be great, attractive pets your aquariums.

> How did they come to be? <

It started as an experiment on a Zebra Danio fish. They wanted to insert the green fluorescent protein (GFP) gene and see if it would glow.
The experiment was a success, and they managed to make genetically modified bioflourescent fish.
They went on to make even more fish glow, and then releasing them to the public, making them one of the first genetically engineered animals on the market.


Now you are able to buy super colourful fishies of many kinds! They recently released GloFish™ brand Betta Fish!
If you want glowy fishes of many colours, I would recommend giving these fishies a shot!
